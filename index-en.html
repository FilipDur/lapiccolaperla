Our Story

A Passion Called Italy

La Piccola Perla is not just a restaurant, it's a dream come true. A dream of a place where authentic Italian food is served, prepared with love and from the best ingredients. Our journey began in the picturesque streets of Rome, where we were inspired by family recipes passed down from generation to generation.

Today, we proudly bring you a piece of sunny Italy to the center of Prague. Every plate that leaves our kitchen tells a story of tradition, passion, and the joy of life. Our pasta is handmade, our pizza is baked in a classic oven, and our wine is carefully selected from small Italian producers. Come and let us take you on a culinary journey you will not forget!
