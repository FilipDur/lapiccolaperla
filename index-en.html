Our Story

A Passion Called Italy

La Piccola Perla is more than a restaurant — it is a dream fulfilled. A dream of a place serving authentic Italian cuisine prepared with love and the finest ingredients. Our journey began in the picturesque streets of Rome, inspired by family recipes passed down through generations.

Today, we proudly bring a piece of sunny Italy to the centre of Prague. Every plate that leaves our kitchen tells a story of tradition, passion and the joy of living. Our pasta is handmade, pizza baked in a classic oven, and wine carefully selected from small Italian producers.

— La Piccola Perla Family
